Ransomware doesn't stop at the edge; once in, attackers move laterally to escalate access. The post describes how zero trust inline segmentation (ZTIS) addresses lateral movement by enforcing identity-based policy directly at access switches, eliminating the need for overlay networks, proprietary fabric, or network access control dependencies.
