Ransomware attacks are evolving faster than ever. The post examines how the Akira and Medusa groups use identity compromise, rapid lateral movement, living-off-the-land techniques, and modern extortion tactics to compress attack cycles from weeks to hours and outpace traditional defenses, arguing that weak access controls and poor segmentation remain the primary vulnerability.
